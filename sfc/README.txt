Projekt do predmentu SFC (softcomputing) na FIT VUT Brno
Prakticka uloha resena siti BP - asociace.
Autor: Pavel Fryz (xfryzp00)

Program provadi uceni na 13 srdcovych kartach z balicku deck1. 
Provadi asociaci obrazku karty s nazvem karty.

Prelozeni programu:
make

Nauceni site:
make learn

Test asociace na ucenych kartach:
make test

Test asociace na jinych kartach:
make test2
